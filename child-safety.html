Child Safety Standards

Yora Social - Last Updated: December 2023

Our Commitment to Child Safety

Yora Social is committed to providing a safe environment for all users, with special emphasis on protecting children from sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We have zero tolerance for any content or behavior that endangers minors.

In-App Reporting: Yora Social provides an in-app reporting system that allows users to immediately report any child safety concerns or inappropriate content involving minors.

Safety Measures & Prevention

Age Verification & Restrictions

  • Minimum age requirement of 13 years for all users
  • Clear age disclosure during registration
  • Age-appropriate content guidelines and restrictions
  • Parental guidance recommendations for users under 18

Content Moderation & Monitoring

  • Automated systems to detect potentially harmful content
  • Human review of reported content within 24 hours
  • Proactive search for known child safety violations
  • Regular algorithm updates to improve detection accuracy

User Education & Tools

  • Clear community guidelines regarding child protection
  • Privacy controls and blocking features
  • Educational resources about online safety
  • Transparent reporting processes

Reporting Child Safety Concerns

How to Report

  1. In-App Reporting: Use the report button on any content or profile
  2. Email Reporting: Send detailed reports to our safety team (see contact below)
  3. Urgent Cases: For immediate danger, contact local law enforcement first

Important: If you believe a child is in immediate danger, please contact your local emergency services or law enforcement authorities before reporting to us.

Our Response Protocol

  • All child safety reports receive highest priority
  • Content removal within 2 hours for confirmed violations
  • User account suspension during investigation
  • Preservation of evidence for legal authorities
  • Mandatory reporting to relevant authorities when required by law

Legal Compliance & Authority Reporting

Yora Social complies with all applicable laws and regulations regarding child protection, including but not limited to:
